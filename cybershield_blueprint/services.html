03.1

Layer 1 · Discovery & Assessment Services

Strategic Purpose of Layer 1

These are entry offers, not the long-term profit engine. Their role is to create trust quickly, expose security gaps, and convert clients into recurring contracts. Target conversion is 55–65% within 90 days.

1. Cybersecurity Baseline Assessment

Scope & Deliverables

  • Rapid asset inventory (endpoints, servers, cloud, domains)
  • External exposure review (open ports, admin panels, misconfigs)
  • Email / DNS reputation check (SPF, DKIM, DMARC)
  • Access control and privilege review
  • Risk snapshot with CVSS-approximate scoring
  • Quick wins list (actionable in 7–14 days)
  • Written report: Executive summary + technical appendix

Scope

  • Assessment against ISO 27001 lite / CIS Controls / UAE PDPL / NCA ECC

Highest Margin Services — 75–85% Gross

These services require no additional infrastructure cost. Pure intellectual capital. Each engagement also deepens the client relationship and leads to SOC upsells. GRC and vCISO services are identified as the most underutilized revenue stream in the original blueprint — now elevated to core strategy.

Payment Structure & Discount Model

Annual-First Pricing Strategy

Never make everything available monthly only. Annual prepay solves your biggest early-stage problem: cashflow. A client paying JOD 4,600 upfront for SOC Standard annual vs JOD 420/month means you have 11 months of runway from day 1 of that contract. Offer meaningful discounts to incentivize annual commitments.
